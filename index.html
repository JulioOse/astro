Summary of Electromagnetic Force, Waves and Light

Electromagnetic forces make objects feel solid because the forces in your hand repel the forces in the object. So because of that you can not push through the object thus that makes the object feel solid.
Waves transmit two things Energy-the strength of the wave and Information-the waves type, style, direction of travel. An important property of a wave is the Amplitude-strength of a disturbance ex.height of a water wave, loudness of sound, brightness of light.
Light is simply an electromagnetic wave. It is only a moving disturbance, it disturbs any electric and magnetic fields present.
